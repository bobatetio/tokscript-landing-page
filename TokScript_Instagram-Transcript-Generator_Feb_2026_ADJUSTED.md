### **How accurate is the Reel-to-text conversion?**

Accuracy depends on the audio quality of the original video. Here is what to expect:

* **Clear spoken audio, minimal background noise:** typically **95%+ word accuracy.** Ready for captions, scripts, and publishing with minimal editing.
* **Moderate background noise or music overlay:** typically **85-92% accuracy.** Suitable for research and reference use. Light editing recommended before publishing.
* **Heavy music, multiple overlapping voices, or low recording quality:** typically **70-80% accuracy.** Best for content research and draft scripting. Editing required before any public-facing use. Transcripts from Reels with clear, direct-to-camera speech consistently require the least correction.

No. TokScript only processes publicly viewable Instagram Reels. Content from private accounts is inaccessible to outside systems. The Reel must be public for the tool to work.

If a Reel contains only music, ambient sound, or visual content with no spoken words, there is nothing to transcribe. TokScript extracts spoken language only, not instrumental audio or sound effects.
