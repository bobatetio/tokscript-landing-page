**Q:** How long does it take to download a transcript?

**A:** The download time for videos transcripts depends on various factors: internet speed, the length of the transcript, and the size or length of the video files. Please note that these are approximate times and may vary based on the specific circumstances mentioned above.

## Notes for Copy Review

- The page accent color is orange: `#F77737` (used for feature group icons, value callout text, and the "Recommended" badge on the Annual card).
- The FAQ component is shared across all landing pages (17 questions total listed in source; the component actually has 18 Q&As in the accordian data array).
- The `ProcessComponent` is imported but commented out of the rendered JSX; it does not appear on the live page.
- Button text on the send/download button is dynamic: shows "Scan Video" when the input is empty, "Download" when a link is present, and "Loading..." during API processing.
- Pricing button states are conditional based on login state and current subscription. All possible states are documented under each pricing card above.
- The disclaimer section in the live JSX uses "TikTok Ads:" as the subheading (not "Instagram Reels Ads:" as in the config file). The live JSX copy is the authoritative version.
- The stats counter section intro stat (`41,000+ users`, `2,600,000 videos`) is hardcoded inline in the JSX and differs from the counter card values which come from `CounterComponent`.
