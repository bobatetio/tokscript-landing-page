### FAQPage Schema
The page includes 7 FAQ items with the following questions and answers:

1. **Question:** How much commission do I earn?
   **Answer:** You earn 40% on every payment your referrals make, monthly or annual. If someone subscribes at $10/mo, you earn $4.00/mo. If they go annual at $39/yr, you earn $15.60/yr. You keep earning for as long as they stay subscribed.

2. **Question:** What is the pay-per-view program?
   **Answer:** Our Track 2 program pays you for creating content about TokScript. Earn $100 for 50K views, $250 for 100K views, $500 for 500K views, and $1,000 for 1M views. You can stack this with referral commissions.

3. **Question:** How long does the cookie last?
   **Answer:** We use a 30-day cookie window. If someone clicks your referral link and signs up within those 30 days, you get credit for that referral.

4. **Question:** Can I promote TokScript on any platform?
   **Answer:** Yes. You can share your affiliate link on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, your blog, email newsletters, Discord servers, or wherever else you have an audience. We have pre-made posts, emails, and scripts in the affiliate dashboard that you can grab and use.

5. **Question:** When and how do I get paid?
   **Answer:** Payouts are handled automatically by Lemon Squeezy via PayPal or Stripe. You can see your earnings update live inside your affiliate dashboard. The minimum payout is $5.

6. **Question:** Is there a minimum number of referrals?
   **Answer:** No minimum. You earn 40% on each referral, whether it's your first or your thousandth. There's no cap on earnings either. Your commissions grow as your referrals do.

7. **Question:** Can I earn from both tracks at the same time?
   **Answer:** Yes. Both tracks stack. If you post a video about TokScript that hits 100K views, you collect the $250 view bonus. And if people who watched that video sign up through your link, you also earn 40% commissions on their payments. The two payouts are separate and both go to you.
