### FAQPage Schema

The page includes 5 FAQ items covering video transcript translation:

1. **Question:** What is TokScript?
   **Answer:** TokScript is an AI transcript platform for short-form video. It extracts transcripts from YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels and gives you tools to download, translate, organize, and repurpose that content.

2. **Question:** How do I translate a video transcript?
   **Answer:** Copy the video URL (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, or Instagram Reels), paste it into the tool, select your target language from the dropdown, and click "Scan Video." You can also re-translate any transcript you've already scanned from your dashboard with one click.

3. **Question:** Which languages can I translate to?
   **Answer:** We support 11 languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese, Mandarin, French, Hindi, Arabic, German, Japanese, Korean, and Russian.

4. **Question:** Can I re-translate a video I already scanned?
   **Answer:** Yes! In your dashboard, any transcript you have previously generated can be re-translated into a new language with a single click. You do not need to rescan the original video.

5. **Question:** Is it free to translate video transcripts?
   **Answer:** Yes. Free accounts get up to 5 video transcripts and 5 translations per day at no cost. For unlimited transcripts, bulk processing, and AI agents, paid plans start at $39 per year.

## Translation Workflow

### Initial Translation Workflow:
1. Copy video URL from YouTube Shorts, TikTok, or Instagram Reels
2. Paste URL into input field on video-translator page
3. Select target language from dropdown
4. Click "Scan Video"
5. Wait while transcript is extracted and translated (similar timing to extraction, approximately 5-10 seconds)
6. View translated transcript
7. Download, copy, or send to AI agents

### Re-translation Workflow (from Dashboard):
1. Navigate to dashboard
2. View previously scanned videos
3. Select video to re-translate
4. Choose new target language
5. Click "Re-translate"
6. Instantly receive translated transcript
7. No video re-scanning required

## Technical Implementation Notes

**No Re-Scanning Requirement:**
- Previously extracted transcripts stored in cloud
- Re-translation pulls from storage

- Key efficiency feature
