### Content Sections

#### 1. Information We Collect
Describes the data collected when using TokScript:
- Account information (email address, username)
- TikTok video URLs submitted for transcription
- Usage data and analytics to improve the service
- Technical information like IP address and browser type

#### 2. How We Use Your Information
Lists the purposes for using collected information:
- Process video transcription requests
- Maintain and improve AI-powered transcription technology
- Communicate with users about their account and services
- Ensure security and prevent abuse of the platform

#### 3. Data Security
Explains that TokScript implements industry-standard security measures to protect user data. Video content is processed securely and is not stored permanently on servers after transcription is complete.

#### 4. Data Sharing
States that TokScript does not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties except:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect rights, safety, or property
- With service providers who help operate the platform

#### 5. Your Rights
Users have the right to:
- Access and update personal information
- Delete account and associated data
- Opt out of non-essential communications
- Request a copy of their data

#### 6. Cookies and Tracking
Explains that TokScript uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage patterns, and improve services. Users can control cookie settings through browser preferences.

#### 7. Changes to This Policy
Notes that the privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be notified by posting the new policy on the page and updating the "Last updated" date.

#### 8. Contact Us
For questions about the privacy policy or data practices, users can contact privacy@tokscript.com.
