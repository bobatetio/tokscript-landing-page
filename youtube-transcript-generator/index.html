How It Works

How to Transcribe YouTube Videos

TokScript lets you generate a YouTube transcript in three steps. No extensions, no software, and no YouTube account required.

Step 01

Copy the YouTube Video or Shorts Link

Open the YouTube video or Short you want to transcribe. On mobile, tap Share then Copy Link. On desktop, right-click the video or copy the URL directly from your browser's address bar. Any public YouTube video URL works: Shorts, long-form videos, and unlisted links included.

Step 02

Paste the Link into TokScript

Come back to this page and paste your YouTube link into the input field above. Need to generate transcripts in bulk? Paste up to 50 YouTube video links at once. TokScript handles each one in a single batch with no extra steps.

Step 03

Download Your YouTube Transcript

Click “Generate Transcript” and your full text is ready in seconds. TokScript cross-references YouTube's native caption data with its own AI speech recognition engine for the highest accuracy across all video types. Download as TXT, PDF, or XML, or feed your transcript directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
