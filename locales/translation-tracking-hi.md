## Notable Translation Decisions

1. **"Transcript" vs "उपशीर्षक":** Used ट्रांसक्रिप्ट as the primary term since it's widely understood in tech communities, with उपशीर्षक (subtitle) used only in context where clarification was needed.

2. **"Hooks" Localization:** Kept as हुक्स (transliterated) rather than forcing a pure Hindi equivalent, as "hook" is a widely recognized term in content creator communities across India.

3. **Bulk Operations:** Used both "बल्क इम्पोर्ट" and "एक साथ आयात" (together import) with "बल्क" more prominent, as it's the standard industry term.

4. **AI Terminology:** Preserved AI agent names and descriptions with localized context to maintain clarity. Terms like "Viral Hook Generator" → "वायरल हुक जनरेटर" keep the English concept clear while using Hindi structure.

5. **URLs & Technical Processes:** Descriptions of "Quick URL Download" translated to explain the concept clearly: "किसी भी वीडियो URL के सामने 'tokscript.com/' जोड़ें" (Add 'tokscript.com/' in front of any video URL).

6. **Modal Descriptions:** Complex feature descriptions broken down with proper punctuation (commas, colons, periods) instead of em/en-dashes for better readability in Hindi.
