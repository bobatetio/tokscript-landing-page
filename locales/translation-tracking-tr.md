- Example: "Panelinize erişin" (access your dashboard - formal)

- "Transcript" → "Transkript" (primary, recognized in Turkish tech community)
- "Download" → "İndir" / "İndirme" (standard Turkish for download action)
- "Generator" → "Oluşturucusu" (more natural than "Jeneratör")
- "Script" (video) → "Senaryo" or "Metin" (context-dependent)
- "Hooks" (viral) → "Kancalar" or "Dikkat Çekici Girişler" (web terminology)
- "Bulk import" → "Toplu İçe Aktarma" (exact technical term)
- "Cloud storage" → "Bulut Depolama" (standard in Turkish tech)
- "Watermark" → "Filigran" (standard in Turkish design/media)
- "Cover image" → "Kapak Resmi" (standard for thumbnail/cover in Turkish)
