## Translation Approach

All translations follow standard French used in SaaS/tech products and are designed to be understandable across all Francophone markets. The formal "vous" form is used consistently throughout to maintain a professional, polite tone appropriate for business software.

---

## Key Terminology Decisions

### Core Product Terms
- **"Transcript" / "Transcription"**: Used as the formal, SEO-friendly term for the primary feature
- **"Transcriptions" (plural)**: Consistent plural form used throughout
- **"Sous-titres"**: Avoided in favor of "Transcription" for clarity and consistency with user search behavior
- **"Télécharger"**: Standard for "Download" actions
- **"Générateur"**: Used for "Generator" (e.g., "Générateur de Transcription")
- **"Script" / "Scripts"**: Kept as commonly used French term (not "Scénario")
- **"Accroches"**: Standard marketing term for "Hooks" (viral elements that catch attention)
- **"Importation en Masse"**: Clear, recognized term for "Bulk Import"
- **"Stockage Cloud"**: Professional term for "Cloud Storage"
- **"Filigrane"**: Standard French term for "Watermark"
- **"Image de Couverture"**: Standard term for "Cover Image"
- **"Favori" / "Favoris"**: Used for "Bookmark" functionality (more intuitive than "Signet" for users)

### Platform/Feature Names
- **"Agents d'IA"**: Translated form of "AI Agents" to clearly indicate artificial intelligence
- **"Rédacteur de Scripts Viraux"**: Clear translation of "Viral Script Writer"
- **"Explicateur de Viralité"**: Translated form of "Virality Explainer"
- **"Tableau de Bord"**: Standard for "Dashboard"
- **"Espace de Travail"**: Used for "Workspace" in collaborative contexts
- **"Paiement"**: Used for "Checkout" in payment contexts
- **"Panier"** / **"Paiement"**: Context-dependent translation

### User-Facing Actions
- **"Commencer"**: "Start" or "Begin"
- **"Continuer"**: "Continue"
- **"S'Inscrire"**: "Sign Up"
- **"Se Connecter"**: "Log In"
- **"Améliorer"**: "Upgrade"
- **"Mettre à Niveau"**: Alternative for significant plan upgrades

### Pricing & Subscription Language
- **"Forfait"**: Standard for "Plan" (Free Plan, Annual Plan, Monthly Plan)
- **"Tarifs"**: "Pricing"
- **"Mensuel" / "Annuel" / "Gratuit"**: Monthly / Annual / Free
- **"Abonnement"**: "Subscription"
- **"Économisez"**: "Save" (in savings contexts)

### Messaging & Tone
- **Direct, action-oriented**: Mirrors English tone with "Commencez maintenant" (Start now), "Obtenez" (Get)
- **Conversational**: Maintains approachable feel (e.g., "Ne manquez pas ça" for "Don't Miss Out")
- **Professional**: Uses formal "vous" consistently across all user-facing copy
