## Translation Notes & Decisions

### Formal Tone (MSA)
- Used formal "أنت/أنتم" pronouns throughout
- Appropriate for professional B2B/B2C SaaS context
- Avoids colloquial dialect to ensure pan-Arab understanding

### Keyword Localization Philosophy
Following the instruction to localize keywords for actual search behavior:
- Native Arabic speakers search for "نص الفيديو" (video text), not "ترانسكريبت" (transliteration)
- "استيراد جماعي" (bulk import) is established e-commerce term across Arab markets
- "التخزين السحابي" widely understood post-cloud adoption in MENA region
- "خطافات" (hooks) is recognized in content creator communities across Arabic-speaking markets

### Feature-Specific Decisions

1. **AI Agents Section:** Translated subsection titles to maintain parallel structure with English
2. **Virality Explanation:** Used "علم النفس" (psychology) + "الخوارزمية" (algorithm) for scientific accuracy
3. **Use Cases:** Adapted "UGC creators" context with "منشئو المحتوى المستخدم" for clarity
4. **Legal Disclaimer:** Preserved English brand names exactly as in source (non-negotiable for legal accuracy)

### Consistency Across Sections
- "نص" used consistently for transcripts/text content
- "سيناريو" used consistently for scripts (video context)
- "تحميل/تنزيل" usage consistent for downloads
- "الذكاء الاصطناعي" used for AI throughout
- "فيروسي/منتشر" - chose "فيروسي" as primary across feature cards
